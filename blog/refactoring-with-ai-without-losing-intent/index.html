Refactoring with AI without losing system intent

Ask a model to “clean this up” and it will. It may also erase a quirk that encodes a regulatory edge case or a performance trick paid for in production blood.

Anchor on behaviour

Characterize first: tests, contract checks, golden files. Then let AI propose structural moves inside that fence. Prefer small vertical slices over big-bang rewrites the model hallucinated as simple.

Keep the story in the repo

Architecture decision records and domain glossaries are fuel for good AI assistance. Without them, the model invents a plausible history that never happened.
