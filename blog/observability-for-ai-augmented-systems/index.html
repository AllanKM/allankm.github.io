Observability for AI-augmented systems

Classic APM shows latency and errors. AI paths add silent failure: plausible nonsense, slow tool loops, and cost spikes without HTTP 500s. If you only watch uptime, you will miss quality collapse.

What to instrument

Model and prompt versions, tool calls, token and euro cost, retrieval sources, confidence or judge scores where you have them, and whether a human overrode the suggestion.

Tie traces to business keys—policy, claim, account—so risk and product can investigate outcomes, not only engineers debugging stacks.

Close the loop

Feed production disagreements back into evaluation sets. Observability without learning is just expensive archaeology.
