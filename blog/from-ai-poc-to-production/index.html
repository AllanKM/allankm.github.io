From AI proof-of-concept to production

The graveyard of AI is full of notebooks that impressed a steering committee. Crossing into production means the same discipline as any other capability—plus a few new failure modes.

Promotion criteria

Named product owner, runbooks, on-call, cost alerts, evaluation gates, data contracts, and a rollback that does not require the original data scientist’s laptop.

If the PoC used shadow data or hand-waved latency, rebuild those assumptions explicitly. Hope is not a non-functional requirement.

Absorbable change

Train the operators. Align risk. Sequence rollout by segment. Architecture leadership here is refusing to scale a demo that the estate cannot yet hold.
