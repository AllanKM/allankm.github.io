Platform teams and AI toolchains

Developers will use AI with or without IT’s blessing. The architecture choice is chaos versus a supported path that is safer and faster than the shadow path.

What the paved road includes

Approved models and regions, secret handling, prompt/version stores, evaluation hooks, cost budgets, and patterns for RAG against sanctioned corpora.

Self-service beats ticket hell. If the official path takes weeks, shadow AI wins.

Partner with risk early

Platform, security, and risk should publish allowed use cases and forbidden ones together. Ambiguity is where unsafe demos go to production.
